# AI Image Generation Prompts for Just Peac Homes

This document contains optimized AI image generation prompts for creating realistic, dual-audience content that serves both investment/practical clients and luxury/custom clients.

## Core Strategy

**Investment Properties**: Use `--style raw`, specify materials, emphasize compliance features (egress windows, natural light), avoid luxury keywords

**Luxury Properties**: Maintain premium aesthetic, emphasize design, use dramatic lighting, showcase high-end materials

---

## Investment Property Prompts

### Legal Basement Suite Bedroom

```

large egress window with natural morning sunlight streaming in, light gray walls, 
38oz plush carpet flooring, white shaker-style closet doors, minimal modern furniture, 
realistic shadows, shot on Nikon D850, 35mm lens, f/8, clean lines,
```

## Technical Specifications

### Camera Settings for Realism
- **Lens**: 24mm (wide spaces), 35mm (standard), 50mm (intimate/luxury)
- **Aperture**: f/5.6-f/8 (realistic depth), f/2.8 (luxury/dramatic)
- **Style**: `--style raw` for investment properties (removes AI over-processing)

### Lighting Guidelines
- **Investment**: Natural daylight, bright and even, realistic shadows
- **Luxury**: Dramatic lighting, golden hour, ambient/mood lighting

### Color Palettes
- **Investment**: Neutral grays, whites, light oak, matte black accents
- **Luxury**: Rich tones, marble whites, brass/gold accents, deep woods

---

## Negative Prompts (Investment Properties)

Always include these to avoid over-luxurious AI generation:

```
gold trim, crystal chandeliers, marble everywhere, glowing neon, 
futuristic gadgets, extremely high ceilings, no windows, fake furniture, 
overly cinematic shadows, luxury cars, infinity pools, perfect lighting
```

## Usage Notes

1. **Consistency**: Use the same camera settings within a project category
2. **Context**: Include "Calgary" or "residential neighborhood" for local relevance
3. **Compliance**: Always show egress windows in basement suite images
4. **Realism**: Use `--style raw` for investment properties to avoid AI over-enhancement
5. **Aspect Ratios**: 
   - 16:9 for hero images and wide shots
   - 4:3 for detail shots and cards
   - 3:2 for before/after comparisons
