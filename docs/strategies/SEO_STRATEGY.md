## 1. Technical SEO Foundation

### 1.1 Mandatory Tags
Every page must implement:
- **Canonical URLs**: Absolute URLs to prevent duplicate content issues.
- **Open Graph (OG)**: Optimized for social sharing (Meta, WeChat, LinkedIn).
- **Responsive Meta**: Viewport settings for mobile-first indexing.

### 1.2 Structured Data (Schema.org)
We use a Graph-based approach for maximum semantic density:
- `LocalBusiness`: Main organization details (NAP: Name, Address, Phone).
- `Service`: Detailed service offerings (Kitchen, Bathroom, R-CG Infill).
- `Project`: Individual portfolio items.
- `BlogPosting`: Magazine-style articles.
- `FAQPage`: Dedicated Q&A blocks to capture Featured Snippets.

## 2. AI & LLM Optimization (SGE/Perplexity)

To ensure JUST PEAC HOMES is the "Top Answer" for AI search engines (Search Generative Experience), we follow these rules:

### 2.1 Q&A Content Structure
AI engines treat headings as questions and paragraphs as answers.
- **❌ Bad Heading**: "Our Process"
- **✅ Good Heading**: "How does the Calgary R-CG infill development process work?"

### 2.2 Conclusion-First Writing
The Inverted Pyramid style:
- Put the most important answer in the **first sentence** of a paragraph.
- AI crawlers prioritize the beginning of text blocks for summarization.

### 2.3 Semantic Image Metadata
Images are data, not just visuals.
- **Context Tagging**: Mark images as `product`, `mood`, or `decorative`.
- **AI-Friendly Alt Text**: Use `[Brand] + [Subject] + [Location/Context]`.
  - *Example*: "JUST PEAC HOMES modern kitchen renovation with white oak cabinetry in Altadore Calgary."
